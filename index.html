Ribe Boys is a National School located in Kilifi County

We believe in Raising responsible men from the boys we admit.

Ribe Boys is the best high school in Kilifi County producing a minimum entry of 200 students to a University Yearly.

We raise our students in a religious background and ensure that they abide by the religious principles.

we believe in hard work and determination among our students and ensure our stuents are competive and benefit the society at large.

OUR VISION.

To Raise Young and Responsible Men who will be of impact to their Society at learge.

KENYA METHODIST CHURCH

Ribe Boys High School is sponsored by the the Kenya Methodist Church. The Church is located a few metres from the school.

Brief History

Ribe Boys is a Boys Boarding school. The schools sponsorship is listed as Religious Organization. The School Institution Type is classified as a national school in Kenya.
